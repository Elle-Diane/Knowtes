Meet Zoid Ham.

Zoid Hæm is an artist living in Denver, Colorado.Know for his fashionable style, unique DJing, and wild parties. Few know he started his creative journey as a writer, publishing his first novel in 2008, "The Bluesin'." This literary work was met with rave and scandalous reviews fromhis friends, peers, and univerisity. He was then forced to remove the book from publication from due to pressure from the administration due to the books explicit content. There was also added pressure from potential future professional football teams who had given him an ultimatum, remove the novel or compromise his chances to advance to the next level of play.
Since that time he has held off publishing another novel…until now.

Knowtes is …

a collection of stories, poems, and essays of a young Black man's journey from ignorance to awareness. Written over a ten year period from college to young adulthood, these notes set a course for the reader through topics like:
Being drafted to the NFL,
Living as a starving artist in New York City,
Throwing mansion parties for the 1%,
Becoming polyamorous,
Growing in the cannabis industry,
Developing his own spritual practice, to name a few. The author finds regue and reflection in words as he progresses through the briar patch of untold perils and strange wonders of life. This book is an account of what it is like to be young, gifted and black in America.
